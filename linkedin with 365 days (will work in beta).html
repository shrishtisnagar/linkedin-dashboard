💾 Where is your data stored?
Everything stays on your own computer, inside your browser. Nothing is uploaded to any server — we never see your data.

Your uploads persist between sessions — close the tab, come back next week, your data is still here. This is what makes the 365-Day View useful: upload a new month each month and watch your trends build up over time.
⚠️ Two things to be aware of:
• Clearing your browser cache (via "Clear browsing data") will wipe your saved reports.
• Different browser or device = fresh start. Data only lives in the browser where you uploaded it.
